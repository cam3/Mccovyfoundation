Our Purpose

Perpetual Ownership & Governance

Established to provide perpetual ownership, governance continuity, and centralized treasury oversight for long-term family enterprises and institutional investment platforms.

The McCovy Dynasty Trust is a long-term private family trust formed to provide permanent ownership, centralized governance, and treasury stewardship across operating companies and investment platforms.

Core Functions

Trust Mandate

Capital Preservation

Long-term stewardship focused on protecting and growing family wealth across generations.

Multi-Generational Continuity

Structured governance ensuring seamless transition and sustained family legacy.

Treasury Governance

Centralized oversight of affiliated holding companies and foundations.

The Trust's mandate is to preserve capital, ensure multi-generational continuity, and oversee centralized treasury governance for affiliated holding companies and foundations.

The Trust does not engage in public solicitation or consumer-facing operations. It functions solely as a private governance and ownership entity.

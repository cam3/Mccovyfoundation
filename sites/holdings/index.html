McCovy Holdings, LLC

Centralized Treasury & Capital Platform

A trust-owned treasury holding company providing centralized liquidity governance and capital deployment across long-duration contracted asset portfolios.

McCovy Holdings, LLC functions as the centralized treasury and capital deployment platform for affiliated operating companies and investment vehicles. The platform centralizes liquidity, maintains treasury governance policies, and deploys capital into diversified long-duration contracted asset portfolios including real estate, royalty streams, and strategic acquisitions.

McCovy Holdings, LLC does not provide consumer-facing services. It operates exclusively as an institutional treasury and investment holding platform.
